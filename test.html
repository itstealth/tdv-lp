STEPS TO APPLY

Applicants can register online with TDV Admissions Portal using their basic credentials. We recommend applicants to use their personal email ID for the purpose of registration, and not a school or office-affiliated email. All communication from TDV will be sent only to the entered personal and mobile number entered at the time of registration.

Upon receipt of the login details from TDV over email, applicants need to login using their received credentials at the TDV Admissions Portal, fill out the Online Application Form, and pay an Application Fee. The form can be completed over multiple sessions, and applicants have the option to save parts of their application as they progress.

ENTRANCE PROCESS: TDV ACCEPTANCE CHALLENGE

The Design Village's approach to admission for its programmes is built upon the institution's core intent to change the paradigm of design education. We are looking for students who not just demonstrate academic excellence, but also exhibit awareness, compassion, courage and perseverance, vital qualities for being a designer.
