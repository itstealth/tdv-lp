INDUSTRY-CENTRIC M.DES PROGRAMMES

MASTER OF DESIGN

Professional postgraduate programme taught with a deep focus on industry-relevant expertise to prepare graduates to work as creative leaders

This programme focuses on creating meaningful digital and physical experiences through user-centered design methodologies. Students learn to design interactive systems, digital products, and communication strategies that connect brands with their audiences.

Key areas include UI/UX design, motion graphics, brand communication, digital marketing, service design, and emerging technologies like AR/VR and AI-driven interfaces.

This programme prepares designers to innovate in the fashion and textile industry through sustainable practices, advanced material research, and contemporary design thinking. Students explore the complete fashion cycle from concept to runway.

Core topics include sustainable fashion, textile innovation, pattern making, fashion merchandising, brand development, and traditional craft integration with modern aesthetics.

This programme develops expertise in creating functional, aesthetic, and sustainable interior environments. Students learn to transform spaces through innovative design solutions that enhance human experience and wellbeing.

Curriculum covers residential and commercial design, lighting design, furniture design, spatial planning, sustainable materials, heritage conservation, and hospitality design.

This programme trains designers to create innovative products that balance functionality, aesthetics, and user needs. Students develop skills in designing everything from consumer electronics to furniture and transportation.

Focus areas include design thinking, prototyping, CAD/CAM technologies, ergonomics, material science, manufacturing processes, and sustainable product development.

This programme bridges design and business strategy, preparing leaders who can drive innovation and manage design teams. Students learn to integrate design thinking into organizational strategy and business operations.

Key subjects include design leadership, business strategy, innovation management, design research, entrepreneurship, brand strategy, and organizational transformation.

This programme offers a flexible, interdisciplinary approach where students combine multiple design disciplines to solve complex problems. It's ideal for those seeking to work at the intersection of various design fields.

Students customize their learning path by integrating elements from communication, fashion, interior, product design, and strategic management to create unique design solutions.
